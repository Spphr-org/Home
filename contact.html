Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a volunteer?

You can go to our “Get Involved” page and fill out the volunteer form or visit our office and contact us directly.

How can I donate?

You will find various payment options on our “Donate” page. You can donate using UPI, bank transfer, or mobile banking.

I want to start a program in my area. What should I do?

Please contact us directly. We will assess your area’s requirements and consider the possibility of starting a program.

Can I help only with money?

No. Besides money, you can support us with your time, skills, or necessary materials.
