Our Programs

We work for community development through various social welfare programs.

Education Support

We provide free educational support, books, and learning materials to meet every child’s academic needs.

  • Free tuition classes
  • Distribution of learning materials
  • Scholarships
  • Computer training

Event Organisation

We organise events and workshops to drive community development, raise awareness, and build social cohesion.

  • Health awareness camps
  • Cultural festivals
  • Speech competitions
  • Community meetings

Cultural and Sports Development

We conduct cultural and sports activities to preserve heritage and promote physical well-being.

  • Cultural programs
  • Sports competitions
  • Theatre workshops
  • Music education

Spiritual Development

We support spiritual growth through prayer meetings, Bible study, and spiritual counselling.

  • Prayer meetings
  • Bible study
  • Spiritual counselling
  • Meditation workshops

Church Member Initiatives

We provide training and support to build leadership and initiative among church members.

  • Leadership training
  • Team activities
  • Social initiatives
  • Mentorship programs

Skill Development and Support

We offer vocational training and practical support to help the poor and underprivileged become self-reliant.

  • Sewing training
  • Computer training
  • English language learning
  • Small business training
