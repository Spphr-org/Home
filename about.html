About Us

Learn about SPPHR’s history, mission, and values

Our Mission & Values

SPPHR Community Organisation is a non-profit working across education, event organisation, cultural and sports skill development, spiritual development, empowering church members, and providing practical support and skills training for people in need.

Our goal is to build a prosperous and thriving community where every member can become educationally, spiritually, and economically self-reliant. We believe a beautiful society is possible through education, spirituality, and service.

Our History

SPPHR Community Organisation was founded on 23 August 2021 as a non-profit dedicated to holistic community development through education, spirituality, and social welfare initiatives.

We began with just 10 volunteers and have since grown into a strong team of 20+ active volunteers.
